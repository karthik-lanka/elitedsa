14. Determine Feasibility to Complete Gas Circuit

You are given n gas stations arranged in a circle. Each station i provides a certain amount of gas, gas[i]. It takes cost[i] units of gas to drive from station i to station i + 1 (wrapping around to station 0 after the last one). You start your journey with an empty tank but can choose any station as your starting point. Your goal is to determine if itâ€™s possible to complete a full loop around the circle in a clockwise direction. If it is, return the index of the station where you should start. If it's not possible to complete the circuit, return -1. It is guaranteed that if a solution exists, it will be unique.

Example 1: Input: gas = [1, 2, 3, 4, 5] cost = [3, 4, 5, 1, 2] Output: 3 Explanation: Start at station 3: Tank: 0 + 4 = 4 Drive to station 4: 4 - 1 = 3; refill: 3 + 5 = 8 Drive to station 0: 8 - 2 = 6; refill: 6 + 1 = 7 Drive to station 1: 7 - 3 = 4; refill: 4 + 2 = 6 Drive to station 2: 6 - 4 = 2; refill: 2 + 3 = 5 Drive to station 3: 5 - 5 = 0 Successfully completed the circuit. Hence, return 3
