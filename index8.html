8. Maximize Profit from Multiple Stock Trades

You are given an array prices[] where each element prices[i] represents the price of a given stock on the i-th day. On each day, you can decide to buy and/or sell the stock, but you can only hold one share of the stock at any time. You can also buy and sell the stock on the same day. Your goal is to find and return the maximum profit that can be achieved by making as many transactions (buying and selling) as needed.

Example 1: Input: prices = [7,1,5,3,6,4] On Day 2, price is 1. On Day 3, price is 5. So, we buy on Day 2 and sell on Day 3 for a profit of 5 - 1 = 4. On Day 4, price is 3. On Day 5, price is 6. We buy on Day 4 and sell on Day 5 for a profit of 6 - 3 = 3. Total profit = 4 + 3 = 7.
