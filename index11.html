11. Compute Research Impact Score

You are given an array of integers called citations, where citations[i] represents the number of citations received for the ith paper of a researcher. You need to return the h-index of the researcher.

Example 1: Input: citations = [3,0,6,1,5] Sort the list to get [6, 5, 3, 1, 0]. Find the maximum h such that at least h papers have been cited h times or more. In this case, 3 papers have at least 3 citations. Thus, the h-index is 3. Output: 3
