7. Maximize Profit from Single Stock Transaction

You are given an array prices, where prices[i] represents the price of a given stock on the i-th day. Your goal is to maximize your profit by selecting a day to buy the stock and a future day to sell the stock. You cannot sell the stock before buying it. Return the maximum profit you can achieve from this transaction. If no profit can be achieved (i.e., if the stock price only decreases), return 0.

Example 1: Input: prices = [7, 1, 5, 3, 6, 4] Output: 5 Explanation: Buy on day 2 (price = 1), and sell on day 5 (price = 6). The profit will be: 6 - 1 = 5.
