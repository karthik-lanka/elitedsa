1. Combine Two Sorted Arrays

You're given two sorted integer arrays, nums1 and nums2. Your task is to merge the second into the first, modifying nums1 in-place so the final result is sorted in non-decreasing order. Assume nums1 has enough space to hold all the elements

Example : Input: nums1 = [1,2,3,0,0,0], m = 3 nums2 = [2,5,6], n = 3 Output: [1,2,2,3,5,6]
