26. Check If One String Is a Subsequence of Another

ou're given two strings s and t. Determine whether s is a subsequence of t. A subsequence of a string is created by deleting zero or more characters from the original string without changing the order of the remaining characters. For example, "ace" is a subsequence of "abcde" (because we keep a, then c, then e in order), but "aec" is not (because e appears before c in the original string).
